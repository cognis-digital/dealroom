MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is between Acme Cloud, Inc.
("Provider") and BigCo Industries ("Customer").

1. TERM. The initial term is twelve (12) months. Thereafter, this Agreement
   shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless either
   party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior
   to the end of the then-current term.

2. ASSIGNMENT / CHANGE OF CONTROL. Provider may not assign this Agreement
   without the prior written consent of Customer. A change of control of
   Provider shall be deemed an assignment requiring such consent.

3. LIABILITY. Notwithstanding anything to the contrary, Provider's liability
   for breach of its data-protection obligations shall be unlimited.

4. EXPIRATION. The current order form is valid through 2026-07-15.
